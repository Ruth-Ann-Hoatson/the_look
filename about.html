The Look Nail and Beauty Bar is a beautiful place with a modern twist that is located in Izotsha on the South Coast. Owned by Nicola king a lovely girl who has a passion for all things beauty related. Nicola is the only person that does all the beauty treatments she is qualified with all her beauty certificates and has been in the beauty industry for 2 years now. With not much experience she decided to take a risk and open up her own salon.

The salon opened up in February 2020 and although lockdown shut the doors of the salon for a few months we managed to make it out and now the salon is thriving more than ever and has gained popularity amongst the south coasters. The salon has a great reputation with a friendly atmosphere. We offer a variety of beauty services at an affordable cost. Our primary mission is to provide the highest level of customer service to each client, thereby establishing a long lasting relationship of trust and commitment. Our expertise and professionalism allows us to provide clients with the utmost beauty experience!
